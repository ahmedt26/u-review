Chris W.

Reason for Visit: Was here in area for a haircut so decided to drop by this famous burger joint.
Menu: Get their mushroom melt! It is ridiculously good. Only thing to comment on is it is a bit pricy for a combo but it is worth it. It is very high quality tasty burgers. The owner/chef is very nice and friendly and you can tell he enjoys his work and has lots of pride.
Service: Great team. Small but excellent service. Nothing to complain about.
Overall: Support local! Great burgers. Great service.

Tanya S.

Best burger in Toronto! A bit pricy but so worth it. Canadian AAA Grade beef, juicy and flavourful. Toppings and sauces are so good. It always hits the spot when I'm craving a good burger. Their regular fries, sweet potato fries and poutines are amazing too. Never had a bad experience there. Friendly customer service. Clean establishment. I've even ordered from Uber eats with them multiple times and the food is still good when it arrives. My favourite is the Royal Z burger (double of course ;) ). Give it a try. You won't regret it.
